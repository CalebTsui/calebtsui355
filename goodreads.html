Connecting Avid Readers Through a Redesigned Goodreads Community app flow and interface

In this 4-week academic project, I investigated an existing mobile app, Goodreads, to identify problems through research and redesign features to improve the user experience.

* This case study is not affiliated with Goodreads as a client and this project was used for educational purposes.

My Role

UX/UI Design, UX Research

Team

Individual

Duration

4-week (Spring 2024)

Background

So...Who is Vancouver Aquarium?

Vancouver Aquarium is a family-friendly public aquarium. It is home to over 65,000 animals and operates 120 exhibitions. Beyond being an attraction, it also leads marine rescue, research and education programs, climate activism, and animals rehabilitation initiatives.

Missions & Goals

Create engaging, educational experiences for every visitor.

Connect people with the natural world to inspire action.

Provide the highest of standards of animal care.
